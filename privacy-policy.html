Privacy Policy for Preloadify

Last updated: 3/17/2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Preloadify ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your data.

Data Collection

Preloadify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data. The extension functions entirely locally on your device.

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • scripting: To inject the instant.page script into web pages you visit
  • <all_urls>: To function on all websites you visit
  • storage: To save your extension preferences locally

Third-Party Services

The extension injects the instant.page script (from instant.page) into web pages. This script preloads pages when you hover over links to make navigation feel faster. Please refer to instant.page for information about their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
